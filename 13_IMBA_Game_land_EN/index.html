Over 300 thousand people watched the Valorant's First Strike in North America

December 5th, 2020

At its peak, more than 300 000 people watched Valorant's First Strike matches in North America. The largest number of viewers for teams from the UK was 7 thousand. The statistics were provided by the Esports Charts portal.

First Strike matches in the European region were watched by 106 thousand people at its peak. Matches of the teams from Brazil were viewed by 55 thousand people.

forZe won the First Strike tournament for the CIS. The champion in North America is 100 Thieves, and the first place in Europe was taken by Team Heretics.

First Strike is the first Valorant championship series organized by Riot Games. The tournament was held in 19 regions.

V's sleeping pose in Cyberpunk 2077 is a meme now

December 9th, 14:52

Players have noticed the unusual sleeping pose of the main character in Cyberpunk 2077. Judging by the animation, V is lying across the bed, and V's feet remain on the floor. This immediately became a joking matter.

Reddit's users started guessing about how exactly V lies on the bed. Jokes range from just awkward poses to the use of a portal gun and detachable arms. One of the users also joked that during the crunches, the developers simply forgot how to sleep in beds.

Cyberpunk 2077 was released on December 10 on PC, Xbox One and PlayStation 4. The title is available on the new generation of consoles with backward compatibility. Earlier it was reported that this release was the largest in the history of Steam.
